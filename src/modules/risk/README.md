# Risk Management Module ⚠️

## Overview
Enterprise risk assessment, mitigation, and monitoring across all business operations.

## Key Features
- **Risk Assessment** - Comprehensive risk identification and evaluation
- **Risk Mitigation** - Action planning and implementation tracking
- **Compliance Risk** - Regulatory and policy risk management
- **Risk Monitoring** - Real-time risk level assessment and alerts
- **Risk Analytics** - Executive dashboards and trend analysis

## Business Value
- **Risk Visibility**: 360-degree enterprise risk view
- **Proactive Management**: Early risk identification and mitigation
- **Compliance Assurance**: Automated regulatory risk monitoring
