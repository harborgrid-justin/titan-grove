# Inventory Management Module 📋

## Overview
Real-time inventory control and warehouse management with advanced tracking and optimization capabilities.

## Key Features
- **Multi-Location Stock Control** - Real-time inventory across warehouses
- **Automated Replenishment** - Reorder point and safety stock management  
- **Cycle Counting** - Physical inventory and variance reconciliation
- **ABC Analysis** - Inventory classification and optimization
- **Lot/Serial Tracking** - Complete product traceability
- **Warehouse Optimization** - Location management and picking optimization

## Business Value
- **Inventory Accuracy**: 99.5%+ inventory accuracy
- **Cost Reduction**: 20-30% reduction in carrying costs
- **Stockout Prevention**: 95%+ service level maintenance
